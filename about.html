About Me

What do you get when you cross a Psychology degree with a UX/UI Design specialization and an endless curiosity to grow?

A UX/UI Designer with a Service Design focus on the end-to-end customer experience.

I've worked on a wide range of hardware and software products, bringing my distinctive user-centric lens to deliver delightful experiences for customers.

My experience includes working two years at Google, where I worked on ramping up the customer experience side of programs, facilitated 30+ design thinking workshops, championed the organization-wide application of user-centric methods, collaborated with cross-functional teams to ensure excellent products throughout the org, performed experience audits and more. I am happiest and most fulfilled when I get to work on products throughout the entire design process, from problem definition to product delivery.

For the past year, my husband and I have been traveling through Europe and working remotely on our company, Sincero , which is a studio through which we offer clients web/mobile design and development, full stack applications and customized tech solutions.

When I'm not looking up the next plant-based recipe to cook up or taking advanced yoga classes, I enjoy reading and taking online courses (currently learning front-end code).
