My Role: UX Researcher and Designer
Timeframe: ~4 months
Client:
Goal: To make fighting climate change accessible, fun, easy and impactful

© 9to5google.com

THE CHALLENGE

I've often found myself wanting to do more to fight climate change. Since big corporations and governments don't seem to be doing enough, it's easy to become jaded about the issue and assume one person's actions don't make a difference in improving our planet's wellbeing. I got it in my head that small yet consistent actions every day could make a huge dent in the planet's overall emissions. I also believed that there had to be other people who wanted to make changes in their daily life to minimize their carbon footprint, so I set out to validate my hypothesis.

MY PROCESS

my double diamond process.

Discover Phase

  • Goal: Understand who my user is and what their needs are for fighting climate change
  • Tools & Methods:
    • Surveys (25 participants)
    • User Interviews (10 participants)
  • Insights:
    1. POC's user is someone who is aware that climate change is real and wants to make changes in their habits in order to minimize their carbon footprint.
    2. Our user isn't sure what the best way to fight climate change is, and doesn't want to depend on governments and corporations to make a difference.
    3. Our user finds it hard to stay motivated and accountable when it comes to fighting climate change because they lack visibility into whether their sustainable choices .
